What is FLOSS?

Free and open source software (FOSS), also known as free libre open source software (FLOSS) and free open source software (FOSS), is software developed by informal collaborative networks of programmers. The source code is licensed free of charge, encouraging modifications and improvements.

About GECW

Government Engineering College, Wayanad is one of the 9 Government Engineering Colleges under Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Government of Kerala. The campus is situated in the side of Mananthavady-kannur state Highway.

FLOSS GECW CELL

Floss Cell in GECW is a group of students who work together to achieve any idea or goal proposed by its fellow members and helps and teach each others in the process
